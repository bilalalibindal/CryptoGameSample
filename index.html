About

Petrol World offers a simple gaming experience where players manage a gas station, optimally aligning it with their budget and economic strategies. Your primary aim is to expand your virtual economy by collecting rewards from your fuel pumps at opportune times.

Features

Pumps are fundamental to income generation in Petrol World. Players can construct pumps using PWL tokens. Once the pump's operational cycle is complete, it provides rewards which the player must collect. After collection, the pump needs to be refueled to start another cycle. Players can upgrade their pumps up to Level 3, further enhancing their income, using PWL tokens.

The refinery generates 'oil' for the player in exchange for PWL tokens. This oil can then be exchanged for Matic that has accumulated in the game's contract address.
